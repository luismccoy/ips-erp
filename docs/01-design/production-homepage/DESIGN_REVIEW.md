## Executive Summary

This homepage is a **solid foundation** but currently sits in "good startup" territory rather than "enterprise healthcare leader." It has strong bones‚Äîgood color psychology, decent typography, clear value props‚Äîbut lacks the gravitas, social proof depth, and visual sophistication that Axxess, WellSky, and AlayaCare bring to market.

**The Good:** Clear pain-point messaging, strong AI differentiation, Colombian market focus
**The Bad:** Generic stock photography, weak social proof, missing enterprise trust signals
**The Ugly:** Testimonials feel fabricated, no real logos, stats lack credibility anchors

## Section-by-Section Analysis

### 1. Hero Section
**Rating: 7/10**

#### What Works ‚úÖ
- **Headline is strong:** "Software que protege su facturaci√≥n" immediately hits the pain point (money)
- **Gradient text treatment** adds visual interest without being gimmicky
- **Stats bar** at bottom provides immediate credibility
- **Dual CTAs** (demo + video) offer choice without overwhelming

#### What Doesn't Work ‚ùå

#### Specific Improvements

2. **Add credibility anchor to stats:** "500+ IPS Activas (audited Q4 2025)"
3. **Clarify currency:** "$5.1M COP" or use billions for clarity
4. **Add a single trust logo** above the headline: "Trusted by [Major IPS Name]"
5. **Replace announcement with real news:** Partnership, award, or certification

## Cross-Cutting Issues

### Photography Strategy: 3/10 (CRITICAL)
The homepage relies entirely on generic Unsplash stock photos. This is the #1 thing killing enterprise credibility.

**Recommendations:**
1. Budget $3,000-5,000 for custom photography
2. Shoot in real Colombian homes with diverse families
3. Capture real nurses using tablets (your actual app on screen)
4. Get 30-50 photos to use across all marketing
5. Consider illustrated/vector style as alternative (like Notion, Linear)

### Color Psychology: 8/10 (GOOD)
Blue + Teal is appropriate for healthcare trust + innovation. Green for compliance sections is smart.

**Minor Improvements:**
- Reduce gradient usage slightly‚Äîcan feel "startup-y"
- Add more white space in dense sections

### Typography: 7/10 (GOOD)
Inter is a solid, professional choice. Type scale is well-defined in design system.

**Improvements:**
- Consider a serif for headlines to feel more "established" (like healthcare incumbents)
- Increase body line-height from 1.6 to 1.7 for better readability

### Mobile Responsiveness: Not Fully Evaluated
Preview.html is desktop-focused. Need to verify mobile experience separately.
