## Photo Requirements

### DO Use ✅
- **Home settings:** Living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens
- **Natural lighting:** Window light, warm tones
- **Elderly patients:** Dignified, comfortable, engaged
- **Technology:** Tablets, smartphones showing apps
- **Colombian context:** Diverse skin tones, local aesthetics
- **Warm colors:** Earthy, welcoming, trustworthy

### DON'T Use ❌
- Hospital corridors
- Operating rooms
- Clinical white backgrounds
- Sterile medical equipment
- Sad/suffering patients
- Stock business handshakes
- Generic corporate imagery

## Color Grading
- **Warm whites** (not clinical blue-white)
- **Golden hour tones** where possible
- **High contrast** for professionalism
- **Consistent filter** across all images
