# IPS-ERP Competitor Analysis
## Colombian Healthcare Software Market Deep Dive

**Analysis Date:** January 2026  
**Objective:** Validate IPS-ERP positioning and identify competitive gaps

---

## Executive Summary

After extensive research into the Colombian IPS software market, **IPS-ERP has two validated unique differentiators**:

1. **AI-Powered Glosa Defense** - No competitor offers automated glosa response generation
2. **Family Portal for Home Care** - No competitor offers a dedicated caregiver/family portal

The market is dominated by Medifolios (900+ IPS) and HeOn/Servinte (enterprise), with several smaller players. **Most solutions focus on EMR, billing, and scheduling** - leaving operational features like nurse scheduling, inventory management, and family engagement severely underserved.
